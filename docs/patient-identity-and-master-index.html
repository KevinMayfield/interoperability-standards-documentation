Current Standards

The current standard for Patient Identifier Cross-referencing in England can be found in NHS England (ADT) ITK HL7 v2 (2.4) Message Specification the section 3.1.4 Patient Identity Management describes interactions used by the majority of English NHS Trusts and suppliers to implement IHE Patient Identifier Cross-referencing (PIX) technical framework.


IHE + HL7 FHIR Versions

Candidate interoperability standards are

  • Patient Master Identity Registry (PMIR)
  • Patient Identifier Cross-referencing for mobile (PIXm)

Combinations of both standards are possible.

PMIR with a single Patient feed. FHIR Cast and FHIR Subscription are included to show subsequent interactions.
