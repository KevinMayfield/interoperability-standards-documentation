Update Encounter (ADT A08) XML Payload PH Payload
Example 1:

Update Encounter (ADT A08)

Scenario: Mrs Fredrica Smith had been admitted to the ENT department of York Hospital having been diagnosed with Streptococcal tonsillitis by Dr Darwin. Upon admission Mrs Smith informed Miss Cortana the receptionist, that she now knew the exact date and time of birth and wished to update the hospital's records. As her mother Helgar had found her original birth certificate, Mrs Smith's demographic record no longer needed to contain an estimated date of birth flag. Miss Cortana confirmed and entered the patient's exact date of birth, and removed the estimated date of birth flag. This triggered a HL7 A08 message to update the patient's encounter information.

Cancel Admit Patient (ADT A11) XML Payload PH Payload
Example 1:

Cancel Admit Patient (ADT A11)

Scenario: Mrs Francesca Smith’s GP Dr Townley decides she needs to be admitted to the hospital for general medical checks and stronger medication. The Malton GP practice receptionist rings up the York Hospital to organise a referral. Miss Cortana the hospital receptionist takes the details of Mrs Smith's surname, first initial, date of birth, street number and postcode. When she searches on the system, she can only find two Mrs F Smith's with the same date of birth and postcode and chooses the first one. The inpatient admission is scheduled with Dr Darwin. Miss Cortana realises that upon opening the full record view which shows full surnames, she had chosen Mrs Fredrica Smith. Both were born in the same month, live at the same address and have estimated date of birth flags. Whoever had entered their original details had set both the patient’s default date of birth the same. Miss Cortana undid the inpatient admission triggering an HL7 A11 message.

Patient Creation (ADT A28)

She arrives at the hospital and speaks to the receptionist Miss Cortana at the front desk. As this is the first time she has ever visited York Hospital, Miss Cortana takes the patient's name, address and date of birth and searches on the Personal Demographics Service for her details. This is found and Miss Cortana confirms it is the correct person. As Miss Cortana had previously consented to having a Summary Care Record, her allergy information was recieved from PSIS and this was also added to the record. Once this was saved and Mrs Smith was registered, this triggered a HL7 A28 message.
